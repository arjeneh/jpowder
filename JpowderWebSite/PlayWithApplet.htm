Demonstrating JpowderApplet

JpowderApplet uses the same base code as the Jpowder application, but displays just one chart. The purpose of JpowderApplet is to allow the display of data, not just as static images, but in such a way, that viewers of your web pages can interact visually with the data. Such plots may for example be used in E-learning. Two examples of this are shown below. If you would like to learn more about how to create such dynamical content see here.

Ways to investigate the plots in the charts below include (for more shortcuts see also here):

  • To zoom hold mouse left click and drag to right and down.
  • To move hold Ctrl and left mouse click and drag.
  • To reset hold left mouse click and drag to left.

The chart below shows exactly the same data as in the chart above but after adjustment the display of the plots. To verify that the two charts do displayed the same data, on the chart below left click over chart and drag left.
